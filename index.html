Welcome to the Columbia University Women's Ice Hockey club team website!

We are the first women's ice hockey team in the history of Columbia University and we are proud to be entering our sixth season! We are open to players at all levels, and encourage new membership. We practice weekly on Tuesday nights and have weekend games at nearby rinks. If you are interested in playing, please contact us.
